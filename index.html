The Man Behind The Mic

WHO IS
MERCILESS?

"Growing up in Mbare where ghetto youths are associated with dancehall triggered my passion for music."

Born Tatenda Charlton Nyevera on July 3, 1990 in Mbare, Harare. Zimbabwe's most dynamic dancehall MC — a rare force who commands the stage, the airwaves, and the dance floor simultaneously.

Hosts Weddy Weddy Wednesday & Caribbean Power Jam on Power FM. Presenter on Jamdown on ZBC TV. MC at Zimbabwe's biggest concerts alongside Winky D, Jah Prayzah & Nutty O.

The Story So Far

THE JOURNEY

2008
Enters The Game
Associates with dancehall under King Labash of "Kukonzeresa" fame. Learns from Major E, Etherton Bennie, Abisha Palmer and Templeman.
2010
Legendary Sound Movement
Joins Legendary Sound — home to Badman Bee, SM & others. Becomes the movement's standout MC.
2013
TV Debut — Bos TV
Presents Rockers Island on Bos TV (2013–2014), earning national exposure and a dedicated following.
2014
OOH WOW EMPIRE Founded 🦁
Launches his empire with DJs Sakhie and Jerry Springer. 10+ mixtapes, residency at Club Red 32 — Harare's most happening Mad Sundays.
2015
ZBC Jamdown Presenter
Joins Zimbabwe Broadcasting Corporation presenting the Jamdown reggae show — reaching households across the nation.
2017
Power FM Radio Host
Hosts Weddy Weddy Wednesday & Caribbean Power Jam. Celebrates 5 years on radio in 2022 with a legendary birthday bash at Wood Pub & Grill.
